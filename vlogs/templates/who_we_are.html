Who We Are

JSBD Overseas Limited

JSBD Overseas Limited is a leading, professionally managed human resource supplier based in Bangladesh. Established in 2018, the company specializes in recruiting and deploying skilled, semi-skilled, and unskilled manpower to the Middle East and various international markets.

With a legacy of experience in the field dating back to 2007, we act as a bridge between the productive workforce of Bangladesh and global employers seeking efficiency and competitive value.

Our Core Identity

  • ✔ Government Approved: Operate under Recruitment License #2375 and proud member of BAIRA.
  • ✔ Proven Track Record: Successfully deployed 10,000+ workers across 8+ countries.
  • ✔ Quality Focused: Training & Testing facilities ensure candidates meet international standards.

Our Values

Our philosophy is built on the principle of "Right Recruitment / Right Placement." We convert human resources into valuable human assets.

  • ✔ Transparency: 100% clear dealings with clients and candidates.
  • ✔ Commitment: Free replacement during probation if standards are not met.
  • ✔ Long-term Partnerships: Equal focus on small, medium, and large clients.
